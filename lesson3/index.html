Site purpose

Sunny Climate is a site where you can check the weather in the different parts of Idaho state and the information about the different towns. these towns are Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. Also, the site will have a gallery with some pictures of different kinds of weather and a form when you can alert about the weather in your town.

Site name

SunnyClimate.com is the name of the site, this name is available to buy, you can see the screenshot of it in the bottom, including the price.
