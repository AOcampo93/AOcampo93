Target Audience

Our audience is the people that out each morning for work, do exercise, go to school or like to do activities outside and they want to be prepared for the conditions of the weather. People that have access to some device like a tablet, cellphone or computer and constantly need to check the weather or they want to know more about the condition of the weather for the next days. Range: 16 to 35 years old.

Scenarios

1. Where can I find the weather in the web?

SunnyClimate is a site when you can find the weather and more information about this topic.

2. I’m living in a cold place and constantly need to check the weather for take my cautions.

SunnyClimate can give you real-time weather and obtain more information about it.

3. I want to go camping and I need to know how is the weather in that place.

SunnyClimate can provide you this information, because we can show the weather in more than one place.

4. I need to go out and train for my next competition, how is the weather right now?

SunnyClimate can tell you the real-time weather and you can check it in all devices (Tablet, mobile and Computer)

5. I’m interested in the weather and I want to find more articles or reports about it, where I can find this information?

SunnyClimate can provide you this information, we are not just the weather website, also we tell you why the weather is like that.
