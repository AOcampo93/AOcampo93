Target Audience

Our audience is the members of the church of the latter days saints that go to the temple and needs some services like accommodate a few days or long stay and wedding reception. These people need to have internet access to generate reservations or see our services.

Personas


Name: Laura & Robert

Occupation: Programmer & teacher

Demographics and Education: They are LDS members and they are going to get married, and they are searching for a place close to the temple for their reception. Both have good work in the city and has a temple very close.

Goals and motivation for using the site: Their motivation for using our site is because there is information about wedding receptions and is very close to the temple. So, is a good option to satisfice this necessity.

Social: They have many friends and family, some family lives far to the temple so, needs a place to sleep on this special occasion. So, is a good alternative to sleep in the same place that will be the wedding reception.

Technology: They are young so, They know all the actual technology and use a smartphone, laptop, and tablet. All devices are common to them.

Scenarios

1. Is there a site where I can find the temple times?

Yes, in this site you can find the times of different temples and different sessions.

2. Is there a place close to the temple for my wedding reception?

Yes, on this site you can find all the information about it. Also, you can generate a reservation for your wedding reception.

3. I have children and Me and my wife are LDS members and go to the temple each month. Is there a place where it can care for our children?

Yes, Temple Inn & Suites has this service and you can find this information on our site.

4. I'm going to go to the temple next week and I need a place where I can accommodate because I live far to the temple is there a site where I can generate my reservation?

Yes, Our site has a section to generate a reservation, just we need to know what day you will arrive and what day you will leave the hotel.

5. I and my wife have a called to serve in the temple full-time. We need a place close to the temple to live. Is there a place to live close to the temple and where I can this information?

Yes, our site can find all the information about Suites for full-time temple missionaries.
