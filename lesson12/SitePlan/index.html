Site purpose

The Temple Inn & Suites is a hotel chain close to the LDS temples, dedicated to accommodate members of the church that will participate in the temple. You can make a reservation, see information about the temples and the times. see the services available in the hotel like a wedding reception and accommodate full-time temple missionaries.

Site name

TheTempleInnAndSuites.com is the name of the site, this name is available to buy. You can see the screenshot of it in the bottom, including the price.
